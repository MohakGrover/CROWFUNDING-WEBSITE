How it Works

It's easy to start a campaign or support an existing one. Just follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Exlore a Campaign

Tell us about your cause and why you need funds. Set a goal and a deadline. Add photos and videos to make your campaign more appealing.

Start a Campaign

Step 2: Why Campaign Is Important ?

The campaign can generate buzz and draw attention to the project, helping to raise awareness and generate support.

Know About Various Campaign

Step 3: Receive Donations

People who are inspired by your cause will donate to your campaign. You can track the progress of your campaign and thank your donors. We will transfer the funds to you once your campaign ends.
